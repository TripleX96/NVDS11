Livelihood resilience

Cooperative farming, savings groups, and clean energy help households thrive and weather change.

Challenges we address

Rural families often rely on low-income farming and irregular labour. Limited access to skills, finance, and markets keeps communities stuck in poverty and vulnerable to climate shocks.

Our approach

  • Vocational training in tailoring, handicrafts, agriculture, and technical skills.
  • Microfinance and cooperatives to start and grow local enterprises.
  • Sustainable farming practices to improve food security and soil health.
  • Market access and business coaching for fair, reliable income.
  • Renewable energy solutions for safer homes and productive livelihoods.
