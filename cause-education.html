Education & skills

Scholarships, literacy groups, and technical training support lifelong learning for children, youth, and caregivers.

Problem

Poverty, distance, and a lack of resources prevent many children—especially girls—from completing school. Teachers often lack training and materials to support diverse learners.

Our approach

  • Scholarships and learning materials for under-resourced students.
  • Upgrading classrooms, libraries, and safe learning spaces.
  • Teacher training and mentorship to strengthen instruction.
  • Community literacy groups and after-school support.
